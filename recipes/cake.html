Cake

Description

In this short guid we will be macking cakes. In the folowing we will mention all the ingredients and the process of macking this amazing recipe.

Ingredients

  • Cake mix
  • Oil
  • Milk
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Start by adding the oil, milk & eggs
  2. Put the mix you just made with the cake mix
  3. Keep sturing until they combine
  4. Put in the oven at 350C for 20min
  5. ENJOY!!
