COOKIES

Description

This is a very simple but amazing recipe, we will be macking chocolate chip cookies. In the folowing we will mention all the ingredients and the process of macking this delicious snack.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose wheat flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Sugar
  • Baking Soda and Baking Powder
  • Chocolate chips
  • Eggs
  • Butter (room tep)

Steps

  1. Add all the dry stuff together (order dose not mater)
  2. Put the eggs & butter in the dry mix
  3. Shape the mix into a pan
  4. Put some chocolate chips to the shapes you made for
  5. Put in the oven at 350C for 10min
  6. ENJOY!!
