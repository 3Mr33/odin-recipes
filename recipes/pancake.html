Pancake

Description

This is a very simple but amazing recipe, we will be macking pancakes. In the folowing we will mention all the ingredients and the process of macking this amazing recipe.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose wheat flour
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Baking Soda or Baking Powder
  • Vanila
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Add all the dry stuff together (order dose not mater)
  2. Put the milk, eggs & oil in the dry mix (in order)
  3. pour into a pan without any oil or butter
  4. flip after a minute and leave it four half a minute
  5. Repeat
  6. ENJOY!!
